Frequently asked questions

If you have anything else you want to ask, reach out to us.

What's different from other apps?

This isn't just another streaming app—it's a hub for all your entertainment. We aggregate content across platforms, so you don't need to jump between apps to find something to watch.

Is Popcorn Time legal?

Yes, it's fully legal. We act as a directory that aggregates content from platforms you already own or subscribe to, like Netflix, Disney+ and more.

Can I manage my own media files?

Absolutely! If you already have a collection of movies or TV shows, Popcorn Time integrates your local library. You can manage, organize, and stream your personal collection alongside aggregated streaming content.

What devices are compatible?

The app works across a wide range of devices, including Android TV, iOS, Apple TV, Android, macOS, Windows, and Linux. It's accessible whether you're on desktop, mobile, or smart TV.

Do I need cloud subscription?

Nope! You can run the media server independently on your own hardware if you prefer. If you like the convenience of the cloud, that option is there too.

What technology powers the platform?

At the core of the app there is a Rust-based engine, chosen for its speed, efficiency, and security. Rust ensures that the platform is both fast and reliable, providing a seamless experience across all devices.

Is the platform still open source?

Yes! Everything we build is open source. This means anyone can inspect, contribute, and improve the platform. We believe in transparency and community-driven development.

Will this app always be free?

Yes, the app is completely free to use. We're dedicated to providing a unified streaming experience for everyone with no hidden fees.

How we handle different media formats?

The backend is built to support multiple media formats effortlessly. We includes a built-in transcoder powered by ffmpeg lib, ensuring compatibility across a wide range of file types.
